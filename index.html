My Forex Journey

I didn’t start with a plan. I started with curiosity.

At first, forex trading was just something I saw online; charts moving, people talking about profits, screenshots that looked too good to be true. I didn’t fully understand it, but something about it kept pulling my attention back.

I was curious, excited, but not really knowing what I was stepping into. I tried to learn first and fast, watched videos, read things I barely understood at the time and on trying it out, I made mistakes before I even understood the basics.

Sometimes I thought I was getting it… other times the market quickly reminded me I wasn’t. But I didn’t stop there.

Slowly, I stopped seeing forex as quick money scheme and started seeing it as a skill, I mean, something that needs patience, discipline, and a different way of thinking. That’s when everything changed.

Now I’m not just trying to trade. I’m building a mindset and I’ve learned that the market doesn’t reward excitement. It rewards understanding. It doesn’t care about how badly you want it but instead, it responds to how well you’ve learned it.

I’m still early in the journey. Still learning. Still making sense of the patterns, both in charts and in myself and one thing is clear: I’m not the same person who first saw forex as just “something”

And like a baby, I’m still learning how to stand in this world; one step, one mistake, one lesson at a time.
